Conditions used to evaluate a loan application

In this section a loan application is evaluated automatically - based on multiple input criteria. For each application will be calculated a rating, which will impact the loan approval, as well as the loan interest - and in the end the loan total cost and monthly payment value.
In parallel with all this, some minimal conditions are checked in order to evaluate if the loan can be approved or not. If one of the following conditions is not accomplished, the application will be refused:
- client's age (if he/she is under 19 or over 65);
- the official loan record (if he/she had important delays for repayments in the past, or did not pay at all);
- the work experience is less than 2 years;
- the client has more than 4 dependents (family members without another income);
- the total monthly payments (payments for previous loans + the ones for this loan) are greater than 35% of the clients net income
- rating greater than or equal to 0.6
If all the exclusive conditions are met, the application will be approved. The default rating is 1, and the default annual interest is 7%. The rating can be improved if the applicant has higher education (Master +0.1, Phd. +0.2), or receives his/her monthly income at this bank (+0.1). The rating is reduced based on age (-0.2 for persons between 20-22 and 63-65 years old; -0.1 for the ones between 23-25 and 59-62), on work experience is between 2-4 years (-0.1), on education (-0.2 for elementary school and -0.1 for high school) and on the number of dependents (-0.1 for 1 dependent, -0.2 for a number of 2-4 dependents).
